About Me

Hi! I'm Thomas Allen. I am a Software Engineer and Game Developer based in San Diego, California. I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Southern New Hampshire University.

I am eager to apply my skills in C++, Python, and software development to professional projects that challenge me to grow as both an engineer and a creative problem-solver.
